Agriculture

Farming with Nature

Empowering Farmers. Restoring Soils. Growing Africa.

Our agriculture division transforms African agriculture through innovation, accessibility, and inclusion. We combine traditional wisdom and modern agritech to create a one-stop ecosystem for smallholder farmers.

Organic Inputs

Bio-fertilizers, seeds and pest control that heal the soil.

Extension Services

Agronomic training and field support tailored locally.

Credit Access

Input financing and flexible credit schemes.

01

Fertilizers

Crop- and soil-specific blends

Our flagship line of fertilizer blends is developed through rigorous field testing. We improve soil health, boost yields, and reduce chemical runoff while being tailored to local crops' nutritional needs.
02

GSI-3P (Produce Payment Program)

Innovative credit model

Farmers receive critical inputs upfront and repay after harvest using their produce — eliminating cash barriers. Example: A rice farmer receives one bag of Urea and repays with two 80kg bags of clean paddy.

Impact Highlights

  • Soil health restoration across thousands of acres
  • Flexible credit eliminating financial barriers

Sustainable Fuels

Clean Energy for a Healthier Future

Bio-Ethanol

Produced from renewable agricultural feedstocks, providing a cleaner, safer alternative to charcoal, kerosene, and fossil fuels.

  • Reduces household air pollution
  • Lower carbon emissions
  • Accessible for residential & commercial use

Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)

Laying the groundwork for Africa's entry into the global SAF market — producing jet fuel from sustainable, non-fossil sources.

  • Significantly lowers lifecycle emissions
  • Utilizes waste and biomass
  • Supports net-zero aviation goals
